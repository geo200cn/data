--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Record layout for midterm exam shape file
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
Data were downloaded from the 2014-18 American Community Survey,
the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, and the city's department of 
public health.
Units of observations: Zip codes
 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data Dictionary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
GEOID: 		Census Tract ID
covidrate:	COVID-19 cases per 1,000 residents 
totp: 		Total population 
popd:		Population density (residents per square mile)
p65old:		Percent of residents 65 older
asth: 		Percent of adults reporting to have asthma
diab: 		Percent of adults ever diagnosed with diabetes
